### 🫁 Digital Twin for Lung Cancer Treatment
> **Personalized 3D simulation of a patient's lung and tumor** generated from real CT scans — letting doctors virtually test radiation, chemo, immunotherapy and combined therapies before applying any treatment to the actual patient.

- 🏥 Simulates day-by-day tumor response to different treatment plans
- 🧬 Predicts side effects without any risk to the patient
- 🧠 Acts as a **virtual safety layer** between diagnosis and treatment
